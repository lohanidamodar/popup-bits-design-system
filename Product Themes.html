Product Themes

Seven products × two variants (safe + bold) × two modes (light + dark) — 28 demos total. Every theme is designed from first principles against its archetype, not eyedropped from shipping builds.

Caveats. All themes are design judgement — accent hexes, archetype pairings, and safe/bold pairings are open to change. Logos are fresh placeholders; swap when officials land.
